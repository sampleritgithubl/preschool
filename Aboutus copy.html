About Pre-School

Playschool helps in building a strong foundation in social, pre-academics, and general life skills. It helps in the development of a child's emotional and personal growth and provides opportunities for children to learn in ways that sheerly interests them and develop a strong sense of curiosity. Consequently, it helps in building a positive association with inquisitive learning in the form of fun activities and guided play. Playschool is important for your child as it helps in making the child habitual of the routine. The child also becomes aware of himself/herself and develops motor and cognitive skills. Playschools further enable the child to receive individual attention as the school has a very low student-to-teacher ratio. According to the report, only 48% of poor children who were born in 2001 "started school ready to learn, compared to 75% of children from middle- income families." Additionally, the amount of time parents of various socioeconomic statuses spend reading to their children has changed since the 1960s and 1970s. Parents with higher education read to their kids for up to an additional 30 minutes per day between 2010 and 2012, which adds up by the time the kids enter kindergarten. The kids are given the 'right' toys to play with according to their age of development which helps them to develop and learn the things that can be implemented or transferred onto them, such as changing the clothes of the doll, feeding the doll, etc. Some more benefits of playschool are mentioned below

About Us

Founded over 12 years ago, our preschool has proudly established itself as a leader in early childhood education, offering a rich and nurturing environment where young learners can thrive. Our state-of-the-art facilities are thoughtfully designed to inspire creativity and exploration, featuring spacious classrooms, engaging play areas, and a variety of resources that cater to the diverse interests of our students. Our dedicated team of experienced teachers, each with a passion for early education and a wealth of knowledge in child development, are committed to fostering a love of learning through personalized instruction and hands-on activities. We are proud to have built a strong and vibrant community of families who support our mission, as evidenced by our ever-growing fan base; they trust us to provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment that not only prepares children for future academic success but also helps them develop vital social skills and lifelong friendships

Vision

To be a leading preschool that inspires young learners to reach their full potential by fostering a love for education, creativity, and collaboration in a safe and nurturing environment.

Mission
