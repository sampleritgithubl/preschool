Frequently Asked Questions

What age group does your preschool serve?

We welcome children from ages 2 to 5, helping them prepare for kindergarten through age-appropriate learning activities and social experiences.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?

We maintain a low teacher-to-child ratio to ensure that each child receives individualized attention and care. Our ratio is typically 1 teacher for every 8 children.

How do you ensure children's safety?

The safety of your child is our top priority. We have secure entrances, monitor children at all times, and have trained staff in first aid and CPR. Additionally, our facility is regularly cleaned and sanitized.

Do you offer flexible enrollment options?

We offer both part-time and full-time enrollment to accommodate different family schedules. You can choose from morning, afternoon, or full-day programs based on your needs.
